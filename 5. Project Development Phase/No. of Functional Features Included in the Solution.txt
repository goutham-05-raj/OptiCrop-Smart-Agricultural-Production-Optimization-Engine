Opticrop includes the following five core functional features:

1. Smart Crop Recommendation Classifier:
   Predicts the best crop from 22 distinct categories based on soil NPK levels, pH, and environmental climate inputs.

2. Expected Crop Yield Regressor:
   Uses Random Forest regression models to estimate expected crop yields (tons/hectare) for the recommended crops.

3. Soil Health Cluster Diagnoser:
   Uses K-Means clustering to classify soil into 5 diagnostic categories, providing insights on soil structure.

4. NPK Vitality Correction Prescriptions:
   Analyzes soil nutrient levels against target ranges for the recommended crop, generating fertilizer corrective suggestions.

5. Performance Analytics & Weather Telemetry Dashboards:
   - `dashboard.html` visualizes model accuracy, feature importances, and confusion matrices.
   - `weather.html` simulates climate telemetry, tracking temperature, humidity, and rainfall trends.
