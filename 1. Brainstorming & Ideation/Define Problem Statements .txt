1. The Crisis in Traditional Farming:
Traditional agricultural practices rely on history or heuristics to select crops and apply fertilizers. This leads to severe operational inefficiencies:
 - Crop Misalignment: Planting crops incompatible with soil nutrient levels and weather patterns, resulting in low yields.
 - Nutrient Imbalance: Over-applying or under-applying NPK chemical fertilizers, causing soil degradation, groundwater pollution, and financial waste.
 - Yield Uncertainty: Inability to forecast yields, increasing financial risks for smallholder farmers.

2. Core Project Objectives:
Opticrop addresses these challenges by developing a smart production optimization engine that:
 - Recommends the most suitable crop based on N, P, K, pH, rainfall, temperature, and humidity.
 - Predicts expected crop yields (tons/hectare) for the recommended crops.
 - Classifies soil health characteristics using unsupervised clustering.
 - Provides automated NPK corrective guidelines to balance nutrients and reduce fertilizer waste.
